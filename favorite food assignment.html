It is our very own beans

Key Ingredients

  • Black eye Beans
    • Alternatively you can use lentil
  • Freshly diced beef
  • Hand Shreded tuna flakes
  • Tomatoes
  • Onion
    • You can add garlic
  • Palm Oil

Procedures

Okay Lets get cooking, i mean the food not us :)

  1. Ensure your Beans or lentils are clean and free of stones
  2. Soak beans of lentil overnight(Makes it cook quicker)
  3. boil water in a saucepan twice the amount of beans to be cooked
  4. Drain water off soaked beans or lentils and pour beans into boiling water and leave for 30 minutes
  5. After 30 minutes keep checking for softening of beans or lentil
  6. Once softening is noticed put palm oil on fire and take beans of fire
  7. When oil is hot, put in onions and stir fry
  8. When onions are about browning, put in your beef and stir fry till properly cooked
  9. Now put in beans or lentils after drained and let it cook and stir once or twice
  10. When ready take off fire and slice tomatoes thinly and serve hot
